# Accommodation – Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to view the answer.  
Use Ctrl + F (Windows) or ⌘ + F (Mac) to search within this page.

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Do students have to live on campus?</strong></summary>

No. Living on campus is optional.  
The decision to reside on campus is solely the choice of the student.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Are there dormitories on campus?</strong></summary>

Yes. The University provides a limited number of residential facilities for full-time students.

There are:
- Seven (7) halls of residence  
- Separate facilities for males and females  
- Limited spaces, subject to availability  

Interested students must complete a Boarding Application and submit it to the Student Services Department.

For assistance, contact the Accommodation Office:
- Email: boarding4utech.edu.jm  
- Extension: 2841  
- Hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>If I live in a dorm, do I have to leave during holidays?</strong></summary>

Yes. Accommodation fees cover only the semester period.

Students are required to vacate the dormitories at the end of each semester.

Students who require accommodation beyond the semester period should contact the Accommodation Office for guidance.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Do male and female students share accommodation facilities?</strong></summary>

No. Male and female students do not share accommodation facilities.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What measures are in place to ensure student safety on campus?</strong></summary>

The safety and security of students are a priority of the University.

Security measures include:
- Security personnel on duty 24 hours per day  
- Electronic security systems installed across the campus  

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>How do international students apply for accommodation?</strong></summary>

International students follow the same accommodation application procedures as local students.

For additional support, contact the International Students’ Office:
- Telephone: (876) 970-5194 or 970-5179  
- Email: jsinclair@utech.edu.jm  

</details>
