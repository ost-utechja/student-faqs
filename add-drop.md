# Add / Drop Process – Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to view the answer.

---

<details>
<summary><strong>When does the Add / Drop period begin?</strong></summary>

The Add / Drop period begins at the start of registration.

- Semester 1: End of week two  
- Semester 2: End of week two  
- Summer: End of week one
</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What happens if I drop a module?</strong></summary>

- The module is removed from your academic record  
- Charges are removed from your account
</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I add or drop after the period ends?</strong></summary>

No. The system closes once the Add / Drop period ends.
</details>
