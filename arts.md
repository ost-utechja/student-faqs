# Centre for the Arts – Frequently Asked Questions

This page outlines the services, programmes, and opportunities available through the Centre for the Arts.<br>
Click a question to view the full answer.

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What services are available at the Centre for the Arts?</strong></summary>

The Centre for the Arts offers a wide range of creative and cultural services, including:<br><br>

- Theatre productions<br>
- Exhibitions and showcases<br>
- Concerts<br>
- Workshops and seminars<br>
- Tours of the Caribbean Sculpture Park<br>
- Student presentations and productions (conditions apply)<br>
- Video shoots (conditions apply)<br>
- Facility rentals<br>

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What is the procedure to access services at the Centre for the Arts?</strong></summary>

To access services provided by the Centre for the Arts, follow these steps:<br><br>

1. Submit a written request by letter, memo, or email outlining the details of your proposed activity in order to tentatively reserve a date and time<br>
2. Receive written confirmation regarding the availability of the space and any applicable contribution<br>
3. Complete the required application form and obtain the necessary approvals<br>
4. Submit the completed application form to the Centre for the Arts<br>
5. Receive an invoice outlining any required payment<br>
6. Submit proof of payment prior to the scheduled event<br>

</details>

<summary><strong>What electives can students pursue at the Centre for the Arts?</strong></summary>

Students may choose from the following electives offered through the Centre for the Arts:<br><br>

- Introduction to Dance<br>
- Introduction to Drama and Theatre Arts<br>
- Introduction to Music Theory and Performance<br>

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Who can participate in the extracurricular programmes?</strong></summary>

Extracurricular programmes offered by the Centre for the Arts are open to:<br><br>

- Students<br>
- Staff<br>
- Members of the wider Papine community<br><br>

Participation is open to anyone with an interest in artistic expression and creative development.

</details>
