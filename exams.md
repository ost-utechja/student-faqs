<details>
<summary><strong>What time period is scheduled for final examinations?</strong></summary>

Final examinations are held during the following periods:

- **Semester 1:** November – December  
- **Semester 2:** April – May  
- **Semester 3 (Summer):** July  

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>When are final examination timetables posted and where can I view them?</strong></summary>

Examination timetables are published as follows:

- **Semester 1:** November  
- **Semester 2:** April  
- **Semester 3:** July  

Timetables can be viewed:
- On the **University Examinations Notice Board**
- On **e:vision** (personalised student timetables)

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What should I do if I do not see a module I registered for on my examination timetable?</strong></summary>

Students should immediately report the issue (for example, missing modules or module code errors) to their:

- College  
- Faculty  
- School Examination Coordinator  

Prompt reporting ensures timely resolution.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What should I do if two examinations are scheduled at the same time?</strong></summary>

If you have an examination clash:

1. Check your examination timetable as soon as it is released  
2. Report all clashes immediately to your College/Faculty/School Examination Coordinator  
3. Report clashes **at least 7 days before** examinations begin  

If unresolved, you may be directed to the **Clash Room** on the day of the exam.

🚫 Cellular phones are not allowed in the Clash Room.

</details>
