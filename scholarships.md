<summary><strong>How do I apply for a scholarship?</strong></summary>

A Scholarship Listing is prepared for each academic year and outlines:
- Available scholarships
- Eligibility criteria
- Application requirements

**Application period:**
- Applications typically open **around the third (3rd) week of April**

**Deadlines:**
- Returning students (Years 2–4): **June 30**
- New students (Year 1): **July 31**

Further details are available on the University’s website.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>If I register with a Letter of Commitment for a scholarship, can I pay at the bank when I receive the cheque?</strong></summary>

No. All cheques originating from a **Letter of Commitment** must be submitted to the **Finance & Business Services Department (Scholarship Office)**.

This ensures:
- Sponsor accounts are updated
- Student accounts are updated correctly

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>If my sponsor commits to paying more than my fees, when will I receive a refund?</strong></summary>

Refunds are **not processed directly** from a Letter of Commitment.

Any excess payment will be refunded **after**:
- The University receives the sponsor’s payment, and
- Written authorization is received from the sponsor approving the refund to the student

</details>
