# Students’ Loan Bureau (SLB) – Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to view the answer.  
Use **Ctrl + F** (or **⌘ + F**) to search by keyword.

---

<details>
<summary><strong>If I have applied for a Student Loan, am I required to pay the ECD?</strong></summary>

Yes. **All students are required to pay the Enrolment Commitment Deposit (ECD)** to access module selection, even if they have applied for a Student Loan.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>How will I know if my Student Loan is approved?</strong></summary>

Your loan status can be viewed via the **Students’ Loan Bureau (SLB) student portal**.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Do I need to notify the University of my SLB loan status?</strong></summary>

No. The Students’ Loan Bureau communicates your loan status **directly to the University**.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>What SLB loan status is used by the University to update my record?</strong></summary>

Loans with status **“D”** are updated on the University’s system.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Will I receive financial clearance once my SLB loan is updated?</strong></summary>

Yes. Once the SLB loan information is updated on the system, **financial clearance will be granted**.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I know the amount SLB will pay toward my tuition?</strong></summary>

The approved loan amount:
- Is outlined in your contract with SLB
- Is updated on the University system
- Can be viewed on the student portal under **“Sponsors to My Account”**

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>Will the University communicate my registration status to SLB?</strong></summary>

Yes. The University communicates students’ **registration status** to the Students’ Loan Bureau.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>How will SLB remit my tuition payment to the University?</strong></summary>

SLB remits tuition payments **after students are registered** and their registration status is communicated.

Payments are made based on the semester invoice.

</details>

---

<details>
<summary><strong>If I paid my fees and later receive an SLB loan, when will I be refunded?</strong></summary>

Refunds can be processed **only after SLB remits payment** to the University.

Students are refunded **their portion only**, where applicable.

</details>
